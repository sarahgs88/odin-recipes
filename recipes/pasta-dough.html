Egg Pasta Dough Recipe

Description

This is my go-to recipe for an egg based pasta dough adapted from Evan Funke. Egg based doughs are used for most filled pastas and are typical of Northern Italy.


Ingredients

  • 454 grams of 00 flour
  • 256 grams of eggs
  • Plastic wrap

Steps

  1. Add the flour to a flat surface (preferably wood) and make a well in the center.
  2. Pour the eggs into the well.
  3. Using a fork, slowly mix the eggs together while incorporating the flour from the edges of the well to form a dough.
  4. Form the mixture into a round dough and knead for 5 minutes until the surface is smooth.
  5. Wrap the dough tighly in plastic wrap and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Divide the dough in two and knead each dough until smooth and the dough bounces back when pressed.
  7. Wrap each dough tightly in plastic wrap and set aside to rest.
